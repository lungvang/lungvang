Internships

Interns are treated as new staffs in Lung Vang. We put them under the real pressures of work, giving them the full instructions, asking them to deal with the problems that scare our staffs daily, shape them in the work process we maintain in Lung Vang. Thus, after the period of Internship, we do believe they are strong and experienced enough to work for whatever company.

Besides, what differentiates Lung Vang internships from others is the total training we offer. Our interns are allowed with professional seminars, business visit tours, etc. In turn, they will run projects and events to contribute what they have learned in Lung Vang for community. We do believe this way will exaggerate the number of talents in Vietnam as a whole.
